Treeliable provides a robust and balanced approach to tree felling in Minecraft.

### Smart Tree Detection
Unlike simple felling mods, Treeliable analyzes connected blocks to ensure you are actually chopping a tree. By default, **trees must have leaves**. If you build a house out of logs, Treeliable won't destroy it when you break a block!

### Dynamic Break Speeds
Chopping down a massive jungle tree shouldn't be as fast as chopping down a tiny oak. Treeliable calculates the total "support factor" (mass) of the tree and requires more chops (time/durability) to fell larger trees. This is handled via a configured **Logarithmic** or **Linear** algorithm.

### Chop Indicators & Visuals
When aiming at a valid tree with a valid chopping tool, an indicator will display on your crosshair so you know exactly what will happen before you swing.

### Mod Compatibility
Treeliable is designed to work out-of-the-box with many other mods and vanilla edge cases:
- Huge Mushrooms and Nether Fungi (Crimson/Warped stems).
- Tools from mods like **Apotheosis** and **Silent Gear**.
- Tools like the Atomic Disassembler (Mekanism) and Terra Axe (Botania).
